Chanakya Neeti

Chanakya Neeti

Chanakya Neeti is an ancient Indian text of life principles and political wisdom written by Chanakya (also known as Kautilya or Vishnu Gupta) between the 4th and 3rd centuries BCE. The text contains practical guidelines for living a successful and fulfilling life, presented through concise sutras (aphorisms) in Sanskrit with their interpretations.
