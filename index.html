About Swamiji

Sri Sri Shankara Shaktananda Saraswati Swamiji also known locally as Guhai Koil Swami has been gracing devotees at Hosur. Having authored 3 major literary works based on Sri Sadashiva Brahmendra, which have been published in the International Research Journal of the Theosophical Society, he continues to play a major role in key social awareness projects.

Sri Swamiji is blessed to have as his Guru, Pujyasri Dandi Swami Hamsanand Saraswati Maharaj a realised avadhuta saint who graced the earth for 108 years and comes from the illustrious lineage of the great Srividya Upasaka & Jyotirmath Shankaracharya Karapatriji Maharaj, and Sri Nityananda of Ganeshpuri. No wonder Sri Sri Swamiji is also blessed with the purnabhisheka of Mother Kali worship at a very young age.
