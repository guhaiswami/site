Guhai Kovil Aavani Bharani Kali Puja

Published on August 25, 2024

On the auspicious morning of August 25, 2024, Guhai Swamiji performed the sacred Kali Abhishekam and Puja at Guhai Kovil, marking the special occasion of Aavani Bharani. The ceremony commenced at 9:00 AM with traditional rituals and devotional offerings.

The temple was adorned with beautiful flowers and traditional decorations. Swamiji conducted the elaborate abhishekam with deep devotion, offering various sacred items and chanting powerful mantras. The divine presence of Kali Mata was strongly felt throughout the ceremony.
