The South Indian Bhajan Samaj Matunga Visit

Published on December 22, 2024

Guhai Swamiji visited the famous SIBS at Matunga, Mumbai and was welcomed with poornakumbha, further he performed pujas and aarti to the main deities (Lord Rama / Sri Rajarajeshwari) and also gave Anugraha bhashanam (Speech).

The South Indian Bhajan Samaj is a renowned spiritual center in Mumbai that has been serving the community for decades. Devotees gathered in large numbers to receive blessings from Guhai Swamiji and listen to his divine discourse.
