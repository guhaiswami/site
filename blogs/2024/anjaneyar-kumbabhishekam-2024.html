Aanjaneyar Kumbabhishekam at Hosur

Published on September 8, 2024

On the auspicious morning of September 8, 2024, Guhai Swamiji graced the Kumbabhishekam ceremony of Navati Aanjaneya Swami temple in Hosur. The event commenced at 10:00 AM with traditional Vedic rituals and ceremonies.

The temple, adorned with beautiful flower decorations and traditional arrangements, witnessed the presence of numerous devotees who gathered to receive the blessings of both Aanjaneyar and Guhai Swamiji. The atmosphere was filled with divine vibrations as the sacred ceremonies were performed with utmost devotion.

The Kumbabhishekam ceremony, which involves the consecration of the temple and its deities, was conducted according to Agamic traditions. Swamiji's presence added spiritual significance to this sacred event, blessing all the devotees who participated in this auspicious occasion.
