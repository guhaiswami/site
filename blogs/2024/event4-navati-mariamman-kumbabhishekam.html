Navati Mariamman Kumbabhishekam

Published on August 23, 2024

Guhai Swamiji graced the occasion of Kumbabhishekam of Navati Mariamman. The sacred ceremony was conducted with traditional rituals and devotion, bringing blessings to all devotees present.

The Kumbabhishekam ceremony, held in Hosur on August 23, 2024, was attended by numerous devotees who sought the blessings of Goddess Mariamman and Guhai Swamiji.
