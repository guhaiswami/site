Ganeshpuri / Vajreshwari Temple Visit

Published on December 22, 2024

Guhai Swamiji visited the ancient Vajreshwari Temple at Virar and blessed the devotees there. Then he visited Ganeshpuri Samadhi Temple of Guru Sri Nityanand Maharaj & performed pujas there.

The Vajreshwari Temple is a historic shrine dedicated to Goddess Vajreshwari, while Ganeshpuri is known for the samadhi of the revered saint Nityananda Maharaj. Both places hold significant spiritual importance in the region.
