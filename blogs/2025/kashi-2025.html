Kashi Visit 2025

Published on March 2025

Swamiji visited Kashi (Varanasi) for his Guru, Pujyasri Dandi Swami Hamsanand Saraswati Maharaj's 1st Aradhana Ceremony, after his Samadhi in 2024. Swamiji also attended a Sanyasi Congregation there.
